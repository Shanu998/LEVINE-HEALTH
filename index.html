Who we are

African Health system is rigged with several

LEVINE HEALTH

Who we are

Levine Health is a company that seeks to tackle
backdrops in health sysytems by initating innovative
solutions focusing on ensuring access to Medical
information, bridging the gap between Patients and
Healthcare providers, chronic disease management,
Outpatient Care, and so on. Levine Health is
leveraging 21st-century technological tools to create
Mobile Applications containing solutions to a myriad
of health system backdrops.

Our Applications

Medicaly

MEDICALY is a mobile Application that leverages technology to

App Features

In-App secured Chat Feature
This feature allows for sending and receiving high speed
messages between healthcare providers and patients,
including sharing of documents.

In-App Video Conferencing feature
This feature brings more reality to healthcare providers
and patients communication by providing audio-visual
connection between both parties.

Remote Health Monitor
This feature allows patients to upload, store and
exchange health parameters data with their healthcare
providers for adequate monitoring.

Electronic Health Records
This feature contains each patient consultation history to
enhance continued care with the same or a different
physician.
